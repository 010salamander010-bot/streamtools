How to Add to OBS

1

Click one of the buttons above to open a tool.

2

Enter your Twitch Channel Name in the setup box and click Start.

3

Copy the URL from your browser's address bar (it will include your settings).

4

In OBS, add a Browser Source, paste the URL, and set size to 1920x1080.
